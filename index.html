FTB Legacy (recommended)

Step 1: Download and install the legacy version of the modlauncher from the Feed The Beast website. The legacy download is located just below the large download button.

 

Step 2: Move the downloaded application out of your downloads folder and onto your desktop. Run the application.

Step 3: In the bottom right hand corner, click 'select profile' and then 'create profile'. Here you must login with your minecraft account. Username/profile name are both your email address.

Step 4: Under the 'Mods' tab, locate the Direwolf20 modpack for Minecraft Version 1.7.10.

CUrse/twitch

Step 1: Download and install Curse from the Feed The Beast website using the large download button. Login.

Step 2: Inside the application, click on the 'Mods' tab then click 'Minecraft'.

Step 3: Click the tab named 'Browse FTB Modpacks'.

Step 4: Search for the Direwolf20 modpack for Minecraft Version 1.7.10.
